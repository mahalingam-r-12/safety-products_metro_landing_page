About Metro Safety Products

A Delhi manufacturer contractors trust — since 1998.

Metro Safety Products began as a two-machine workshop in Peeragarhi, supplying helmets to nearby Delhi contractors. Twenty-seven years on, we operate a dedicated safety-belt manufacturing unit in Okhla Industrial Area Phase II — with injection-moulding, webbing cutting, and stitch-testing lines under one roof.

Our belts and harnesses are approved under IS 3521 and supplied at scale to Indian Railways zones, NTPC, BHEL, L&T, Ahluwalia Contracts, Reliance Infra and Ambuja Group. We also export to Nepal, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and the Middle East under FOB Delhi / Nhava Sheva.

We do not do retail. Everything we ship is bulk — minimum order quantities, OEM branding, and dispatch paperwork that passes an audit the first time.

FAQ · Bulk buyer questions

Answers for procurement.

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for safety belts?

Standard MOQ is 100 pieces per variant. OEM / custom-branded orders start at 500 pieces. Mixed-SKU bulk orders are welcome — share your list on WhatsApp for a combined quote.

Are your safety belts IS 3521 certified?

Yes — all harnesses and lanyard belts conform to IS 3521 (2021). Shock-absorbing lanyards are additionally tested to EN 355. Test certificates are dispatched with every bulk order.

Do you offer OEM / custom-branding on safety belts?

Yes. Screen-printed or heat-transferred logo on webbing, custom webbing colour, and custom label tags are offered. Tooling/setup is free above 1,000-piece orders.

What are your delivery timelines for bulk orders?

Ready stock: 24–48 hours of PO. OEM / customised orders: 10–18 working days depending on webbing colour and branding. Pan-India dispatch via surface or air, export FOB Delhi / Nhava Sheva.

Can you supply to Railways, NTPC, L&T and similar PSUs?

Yes — we are an approved vendor for multiple Indian Railways zones, NTPC, BHEL, Ahluwalia Contracts, Reliance Infra and Ambuja. Assistance with GeM registration / PSU onboarding is provided on request.

Do you welcome dealer / distributor enquiries?

Yes — dealer enquiries are welcome across India, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and GCC. Territory protection, dealer margins, and POS material are all available.

What payment terms do you offer?

Standard: 50% advance, 50% against proforma before dispatch. Approved PSU / corporate: 30-day credit against PO. Exports: 30% advance, balance against BL copy.
